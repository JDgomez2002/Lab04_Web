The Uncertain Path

Welcome to The Uncertain Path...
In this game the're actions and paths.
- An 'action' give you clues to choose the next correct path.
- The 'Paths' are the ways that you can take to complete the level.
We recomend always look at the actions first. If not, it can end in a catastrophe.

- At your back, there is a dark forest with tall trees that don't let you to see through it.
- At your left, are some ravens that are staring at you. It seems like they're protecting something...
